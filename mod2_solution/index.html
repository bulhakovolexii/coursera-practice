Term cards

Hypothesis

A prediction of whether the independent variable will have an effect on the dependent variable or a prediction of the nature of the effect.

Double-Blind

When neither the researcher nor the subject knows whether the subject is receiving the treatment or a placebo. "Blinding" helps reduce biased results.

Science

Serendipity is an unplanned fortunate discovery; is a common occurrence throughout the history of product invention and scientific discovery. Serendipity is also seen as a potential design principle for online activities that would present a wide array of information and viewpoints, rather than just re-enforcing a user's opinion.
